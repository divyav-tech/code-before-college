What is a Computer?

A computer is an advanced electronic device which helps us perform a variety of functions such as heavy calculations, accessing internet, listening to music, playing games etc.

Key Components & Functions:

  • Input: Receiving data from devices like keyboards, mice, or sensors.
  • Processing: The CPU (central processing unit) manipulates data, executing programs stored in memory.
  • Storage: Data is held in memory (RAM) and stored permanently on hard drives or SSDs.
  • Output: Presenting processed information via screens, printers, or audio.

Types of Computers?

There are various types of computers such as mobile devices, personal computers, supercomputers, servers etc.

Evolution of computers:

The development of computers has seen a transition from massive vacuum tube machines in the 1940s to compact, powerful transistors and integrated circuits, and now to AI-optimized processors, creating a technological shift that allows for incredible speed and efficiency.

Uses Of Computers:

Computers are integral to modern life and use for a variety of different things such as
email, social media, video conferencing, video or photo editing, gaming, music, storing files, database creation etc.
